Chronic Care Management

Streamlining Chronic Care Management for Enhanced Virtual Healthcare

At Health Square, we automate the tracking of virtual care, simplify care plan assignment, and seamlessly capture all your billable metrics in one centralized system.

How We Enhance Your Chronic Care Management (CCM) Program

1. Automate Virtual Care Time Tracking Automatically track every minute of non-face-to-face patient care by patient, healthcare provider, and task, ensuring seamless virtual care time management.

2. Improve Patient Care Easily assign advanced clinical care guides, receive automated reminders for key events, and ensure consistent care plan adherence across your healthcare team.

3. Increase Revenue Generation Quickly identify billable patients within any time period and monitor those approaching billable event thresholds to maximize healthcare billing efficiency.
